Who I Am
A developer who thinks
like a designer.

Hey — I'm Muhammad Ikhsan, a frontend web developer and UI/UX enthusiast based in Indonesia. Currently studying Computer Science and spending most of my time between VS Code and Figma.

My obsession is the intersection of clean code and beautiful interfaces. I believe great software should feel as good as it works — every pixel, every interaction, every transition should carry intention.

I specialize in React + TypeScript applications and love translating design systems into living, breathing UI — from habit trackers to dashboards, always chasing that "this feels right" quality.

Outside of code I think about productivity systems, human-computer interaction, and why most enterprise software is designed to demoralize. I'd like to fix that, one component at a time.
